## Don't trust untrusted repos

Cloned repo from someone you don't know? Malicious `CLAUDE.md` or hooks can trick Claude into harmful commands.

Stick to repos you trust. Read `CLAUDE.md` and `.claude/settings.json` before running Claude in a fresh clone.
