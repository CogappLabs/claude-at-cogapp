## Tips

1. **Be specific**: vague in, vague out
2. **Give context**: files, errors, expectations
3. **Iterate**: say what's wrong; don't restart
4. **`/clear` between tasks**
5. **Read what it writes**
6. **Start small**: bug fix before new feature
