## Cost mental model

Rough ballpark per Cogapp dev:

- **Casual use**: £5–20/mo (Pro covers it)
- **Daily, Sonnet-heavy**: Pro plan, occasional overage
- **Heavy multi-agent + Opus**: Max or Team
- **1M context, big codebases**: Team / Enterprise

A focused 1-hour Sonnet session ≈ pence. Opus 1M context refactor ≈ £1–3.
