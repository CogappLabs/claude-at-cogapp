## Context & tokens

- **Context window** = how much Claude sees at once
- **Tokens** ≈ 4 characters each
- Older parts compress as it fills
- `/clear` between unrelated tasks. Fresh context = better answers.

### Why `/clear` helps

- Stale facts stop getting cited (old files, old wrong turns)
- Earlier framing stops biasing later answers
- Tool/file state assumptions reset
- Lower token cost: no replay of old turns

Same model, cleaner signal, sharper answers.
